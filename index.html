Aircraft Of The Week (April 12-19, 2026)

Aircraft: Airbus A319

Model: Airbus A319-115(CJ)

Registration: HZ-CMJ

Operator: Alpha Star Aviation

Airframe Age: ~14.8 Years

Engines: 2x CFMI CFM56-5B5/3

The Airbus A319CJ (Corporate Jet) is a high-performance VIP variant of the A319 airliner, designed for ultra-long-range travel with a luxurious, spacious cabin. It typically seats 8 to 19 passengers (up to 48 in some configurations) and features a range of up to 6,000 nautical miles, capable of non-stop flights like London to New York.
HZ-CMJ is an Airbus A319-115(CJ) VIP corporate jet (MSN 4768) operated by Alpha Star Aviation Services, based in Saudi Arabia. Originally produced in 2011, it features a VIP configuration and is actively used for executive transport, frequently traveling between destinations like JFK, Shannon, and the Middle East. It was formerly registered as VP-CMJ.
