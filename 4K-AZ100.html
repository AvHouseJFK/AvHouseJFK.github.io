Registration: 4K-AZ100

The aircraft involved in this visit was a Silk Way Azerbaijan Cargo Ilyushin IL-76TD-90VD, a heavy lift cargo aircraft designed for large scale freight operations and specialized missions. The IL-76 is known for its rugged design and ability to operate in challenging environments.

Flight Leg 1

Flight Number: AZQ2223 (Silk Line 2223)

Departure Time: April 17, 2026 at 3:25 AM UTC

Arrival Time: April 17, 2026 at 6:21 AM EDT

The departure from Keflavík occurred 25 minutes later than scheduled. Despite the delay, the flight proceeded normally across the North Atlantic before arriving in New York. The aircraft landed at JFK 27 minutes later than originally planned.

Ground Operations at JFK

After arrival, the aircraft was parked at Cargo Building 23 in Area B. It remained on the ground for approximately 12 hours and 32 minutes. During this time, ground crews handled unloading and preparation for the next sector.

The extended ground time is typical for specialized cargo missions, especially when handling oversized freight or mission specific equipment.

Overview of the Visit

This visit of a Silk Way Azerbaijan Cargo Ilyushin IL-76TD-90VD to JFK Airport was a notable aviation event. Aircraft of this type are not commonly seen at major commercial airports in the United States. When they do appear, they are usually operating under special charter agreements rather than regular cargo schedules.

The aircraft arrived in the early morning hours after a transatlantic flight from Iceland. It completed its cargo handling operations over the course of the day before departing in the evening to Houston. The entire operation reflected the flexibility required in international cargo logistics.

Aircraft Characteristics

The Ilyushin IL-76 is a Soviet designed heavy transport aircraft originally developed for military and cargo use. The TD-90VD variant is optimized for civilian cargo operations and long range international missions.

It is capable of carrying oversized loads, heavy equipment, and specialized freight that may not fit into standard cargo aircraft. Its robust landing gear and high mounted wings allow it to operate from a variety of runway conditions.

Operator Background

Silk Way Airlines is a cargo operator based in Baku, Azerbaijan. The airline focuses on heavy lift cargo, charter missions, and government supported logistics operations.

Unlike major scheduled cargo carriers, Silk Way operates on a flexible routing model. Flights are arranged based on contract demand rather than fixed timetables. This makes its operations less predictable but highly specialized.

Because of this operational model and its limited fleet of Il-76 aircraft, appearances at airports like JFK are relatively rare and often draw attention from aviation enthusiasts and spotters.
